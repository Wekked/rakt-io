The Problem

Restaurant inventory is still stuck in the '90s.

 📋

Pen & Paper

Staff counts on clipboards, then someone re-types everything into a spreadsheet. Errors multiply at every step.

 💰

$200+/Month Software

Enterprise tools require long contracts and complex onboarding. Overkill for a single location.

 📡

No Signal Where It Matters

Walk-in freezers, basements, and dry storage are dead zones. Cloud-only apps fail exactly where you count.

 🖥

Not Built for Kitchens

Desktop-first apps with tiny buttons don't work on iPads with wet hands. Staff need big tap targets and simple flows.

The Solution

Purpose-built for the way restaurants actually work.

Real-Time Cloud Sync

Every device sees the same data. Changes sync instantly across all logged-in users.

Key Differentiator

Offline-First

Count your entire walk-in without a bar of service. Everything queues locally and syncs when back online.

iPad Optimized

Big tap targets, swipe navigation, add-to-home-screen. Designed for wet hands and fast-paced kitchens.

Multi-User Roles

Admin, Manager, and Staff logins. Control who can edit items vs. just count.

Custom Walkthrough Order

Drag-and-drop reorder to match your physical shelves. No more hunting for items.

Value Tracking & Export

See inventory value by location and category. Export CSV for accounting.
